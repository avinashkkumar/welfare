Girl child protection

Mukhyamantri Rajshri Yojana

This scheme provides financial assistance to orphaned girl children, including those who have lost their parents due to accidents, natural disasters, or other unfortunate circumstances. It aims to support their education, healthcare, and overall development.

Childline Services

Childline is a 24-hour toll-free helpline (1098) that provides assistance and support to children in distress. It offers immediate rescue and protection services to girl children facing abuse, exploitation, or any form of danger.
