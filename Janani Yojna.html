Protection of Janani Yojna

1. Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)

Launched in 2005, JSY is a safe motherhood intervention under the National Health Mission. It promotes institutional deliveries by providing financial assistance to pregnant women, including those facing pressure or coercion for abortion. The scheme aims to reduce maternal and infant mortality.

2. Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

PMMVY, introduced in 2017, offers financial support to pregnant and lactating women to ensure good nutrition and proper care during pregnancy and after childbirth. This initiative encourages women to make informed choices regarding their pregnancies and maternal health.

3. Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS)

ICDS is a comprehensive program that provides various services, including nutrition and healthcare, to pregnant women, lactating mothers, and children below the age of six. It indirectly helps in promoting women's health and reducing instances of forced abortions.

4. One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme

The One Stop Centre (OSC) Scheme, launched as part of the Nirbhaya Fund in 2015, establishes centers that provide integrated support and assistance to women affected by violence, including cases of forced abortion. These centers offer medical, legal, psychological, and counseling services to women in distress.

5. Ujjawala Scheme

The Ujjawala Scheme, initiated in 2007, focuses on the rescue and rehabilitation of women who are victims of trafficking, including forced prostitution. While not specifically addressing forced abortion, this scheme provides support and protection to vulnerable women and girls who may face coercion and exploitation.
